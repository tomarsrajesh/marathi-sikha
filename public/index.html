🙏 About मराठी शिका

A free app to help anyone learn Marathi — spoken by 83+ million people. Made with ❤️ by @mainormerimumbai.

✨ How to use

  • Type in English, Hindi or Hinglish
  • Or tap 🎤 and speak
  • Tap story circles for topic-wise phrases
  • Tap 🔊 to hear pronunciation

📲 Add to Home Screen

Save this app on your phone — works like a real app!

🤖 Android Chrome:

1. Tap ⋮ menu (top right in Chrome)
2. Tap "Add to Home screen"
3. Tap "Add" ✅

🍎 iPhone Safari:

1. Tap Share ↑ button at bottom
2. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
3. Tap "Add" ✅
